A Rated Washing Machines

Haier's Direct Motion Motors have been designed and built to last with a lifetime guarantee. Operating without a belt, the motor is directly attached to the drum, vastly reducing noise and vibration, increasing durability while also reducing energy and water consumption. All of our washing machines are A-rated for energy which means that less electricity is used over the course of washes, saving you over £100**, and also benefitting the environment.

**Washing energy saving based on 3 years, comparing an A rated Haier washing machine vs the market average model with corresponding KG load size on D or E energy rating. Cost of energy based on 34p per kw/h, 284 uses per year.
